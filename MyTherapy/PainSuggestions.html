Example template (permissive / indirect) — knee pain

“You may notice changes in your knee, or you may simply notice how your attention relates to it for now. It can be interesting to observe whether the sensation stays the same or shifts in some small way. Sometimes comfort develops gradually, and sometimes the first sign is simply noticing something different. You might become curious about what your knee does when nothing is being forced. The intention can be to allow comfort, while your body decides how that shows up. Different people notice different responses, and whatever you notice is useful information. Even noticing moments when the knee matters less can be a meaningful shift. And if nothing changes right now, that’s simply part of learning how your system responds.”
