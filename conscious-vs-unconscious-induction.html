Simplified Induction Script

Your conscious mind observes.
Your unconscious mind runs.

Your conscious mind plans.
Your unconscious mind knows.

Your conscious mind thinks.
Your unconscious mind feels.

Your conscious mind listens.
Your unconscious mind understands.

Your conscious mind looks.
Your unconscious mind sees within.

Your conscious mind remembers some things.
Your unconscious mind remembers everything that matters.

Your conscious mind forgets what is not needed.
Your unconscious mind keeps what is useful.

Your conscious mind focuses on details.
Your unconscious mind notices the whole.

Your conscious mind asks how.
Your unconscious mind already knows how.

Your conscious mind sleeps and wakes.
Your unconscious mind never sleeps.

Your conscious mind tries.
Your unconscious mind allows.

Your conscious mind is aware.
Your unconscious mind is deeper.

And as your conscious mind listens,
your unconscious mind can respond.

And as you consider the conscious mind…
versus your unconscious…
you can easily and naturally go to sleep now.
(Snap)
Eyes close… now.
