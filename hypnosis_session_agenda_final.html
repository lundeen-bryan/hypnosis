Seven Step Hypnosis Session Agenda

Modified from Mike Mandel's Hypnosis Academy (MMHA) Lesson 8 for student practice sessions to include a post‑talk section.

Quick Checklist

1) Demystify Hypnosis (Pretalk)

  • Hypnosis is natural, safe, and cooperative; the subject stays in control.
  • No “stuck,” no forced secrets, no acting against values.
  • Invite simple compliance: “Just follow along and allow it to work.”

2) Suggestibility Tests (Not used in the Ericksonian model)

Direct model: typically use a quick test. Ericksonian model: typically not used (per MMHA).

  • Hand clasp test
  • Finger magnets
  • Arm levitation micro‑test

3) Induction (general)

  • Choose a simple induction you like:
    • Eye fixation
    • Spiegel Eye Roll
    • Progressive relaxation
  • Keep it short and smooth; steady tone and pacing.

4) Deepeners

  • Mandel Unconscious Deepener
  • Counting down / staircase / elevator
  • Fractionation (“open… close… deeper”)

Pick one when you want more stability or practice variety.

5) Convincers — Pick 1–2

6) Change Work or Positive Suggestion

Pick 1–2 simple, student‑friendly lines to deliver during trance.

7) Awaken / Emergence (brief)

  • “In a moment I’ll count 1–5. At 5 you’re clear, refreshed, and alert.”
  • Re‑orient: open eyes, stretch, sip water. Confirm they feel balanced.

Post‑Talk (Reflection)

After‑Effects to Notice (after emergence)

  • Watery/glassy eyes or blinking changes
  • Brief amnesia for parts of the process
  • Lingering catalepsy (eyes/arm prefer to stay still)
  • Time distortion report
  • Slowed, steady breathing; softer facial tone
  • A convincer worked as expected
