Flow for Each Person

  1. Opening (1–2 min) — quick check-in; confirm roles.
  2. Suggestion Menu Selection (≈5 min) — subject consciously chooses 1–2 suggestions before trance.
  3. Pre-Talk Practice (≈5 min) — brief explanation, set expectations, answer questions.
  4. Induction (≈10 min) — any style (eye-fixation, relaxation, Ericksonian), smooth pacing.
  5. Deepening & Delivery (10–15 min) — deepen and weave in the chosen suggestion(s).
  6. Emergence (≈5 min) — reorient, count up, ensure alertness and comfort.
  7. Debrief (≈8–10 min) — subject shares experience; hypnotist reflects; set a micro-goal.
Remember: speak suggestions in second-person, post-hypnotic style (e.g., “When you wake up, you will…”).

Suggestion Menu

    Debrief Prompts

    • What moments felt most effective or natural?
    • What could be smoother in pacing, tone, or word choice?
    • Did the suggestion feel congruent and relevant?
    • Any signs of trance depth you noticed (breath, eye changes, response lag)?
    • One takeaway and one improvement goal for next session.
