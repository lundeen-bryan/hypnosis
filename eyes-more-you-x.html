Eyes Open → Utilization Flows

How to use

Tap any flow when a subject's eyes won’t close. Each line softly links what they’re doing (keeping eyes open) to what you want (closing/going inward), using the “the more you X, the more you Y” pattern.

Flow 1 — Heaviness → Effort → Rest
That’s right… and the more your eyes want to stay open, the heavier they begin to feel… And the more effort it takes to keep them open, the quicker that effort just turns into a comfortable tiredness… And as that tiredness spreads, your eyes may simply decide for themselves to rest… gently closing now.

Flow 2 — Blink → Curiosity → Focus
You may notice… the more you keep them open, the more little blinks want to happen… And the more you notice those blinks, the more curious a part of you becomes about just letting them close… And the more they close, even slightly, the more your focus can drift inward… into that deeper calm waiting for you now.
Expand
Flow 3 — Effort → Heaviness → Relaxation
The more you try to keep them open, the more effort it takes… And the more effort you feel, the heavier those eyelids can become… And the heavier they feel, the more relaxed your whole body can begin to feel… almost as if closing is the most natural thing in the world now.
Expand

Tip: Speak slowly. Allow micro-pauses after each line so their experience can catch up to your words.
