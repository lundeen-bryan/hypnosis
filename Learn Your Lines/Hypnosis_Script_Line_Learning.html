Hypnosis Script — Line‑Learning Edition (Cleberg)

Each row is a “micro‑folder.” Practice 3× Read → 3× Recall → 3× Perform.

▶I. Pre-frame
I.1
Some people are cat people, and some people are dog people. But I think the main difference between them is how they relax.
I.2
When a cat relaxes, it's almost like it's about to go into trance.
As you sit there now, you might picture that kind of relaxation
I.3

as a helpful reminder of the kind of relaxation
you might want to acheive.

▶II. The Cat Induction
II.1
Without needing to do anything special, you can let your eyes find a spot somewhere in front of you and focus your attention on that spot as you listen to my voice.
II.2
As you're staring at that spot... sitting there in that chair... listenting to the sound of my voice...
II.3
In your mind's eye, you might begin to imagine a cat laying down getting ready to relax... or it might not be laying down yet.
II.4
You can begin to notice how easy it is to picture that cat... And this cat is beginning to feel soooooo relaxed.
II.5
I don't know whether it's eyes might close now, or it might close later, but each blink seems to be slower and slower as if time itself has begun to slow down.
II.6
And the more you try not to think about the eyes of this cat blinking, the more you might feel an urge to let your own eyes blink.
II.7
And I don't know if you can imagine those calm and relaxing breaths the cat might be making as it relaxes deeper and deeper...But I wonder if you realize just how relaxed it's starting to make you feel...
II.8
You might find yourself begining to wonder whether this imaginary cat is helping you become more and more relaxed with each breath.
II.9
You can continue right on relaxing with each breath as you imagine the eyes of that cat closing. Because it's not necessary for you to close your eyes too, but if you do, you can experience a deep feeling of peace and relaxation.
II.10
The speed at which you close your eyes indicates the speed at which you wish to go into trance.
▶III. Eye Catalepsy Convincer
III.1
And as those eyelids settle down, you might find that they remain comfortably closed… and every time you repeat that movement, they close even more easily… until they simply prefer to stay closed… allowing you to go deeper and deeper inside.
III.2

In a moment — but not yet — I'll ask you to gently test your eyelids. Just to see if they might want to open.

You might find that they stay comfortably closed… or they may open for a moment, before closing again and you become even more relaxed.

And you might find that the more you try to open them, the more easily they prefer to stay closed.

You can try to test them now… that’s right… and then simply let them relax completely… and enjoy that gentle comfort.

That’s right… when your eyes open, it simply gives you the chance to close them again and drift even deeper into that comfortable state of relaxation. You can imagine the tiny muscles around your eyes becoming twice as relaxed as before… just by gently closing them a little tighter for a moment… and then allowing them to loosen, soften, and relax completely again… feeling that ease spreading even more deeply through your whole body now.

▶IV. External Sounds Suggestion
IV.1
And isn’t it interesting how, the very moment our eyes remain closed, your attention is automatically drawn inward… as though the world outside simply fades, and what matters most is the comfort within.
IV.2
And any sounds you may hear, or any interruptions or distractions that might occur, can simply be used by your mind as gentle reminders to bring your attention back inward… just as it is now… so that all you really need to notice is the sound of my voice guiding you deeper.
▶V. Story Escalator Deepener
V.1
And as those eyelids rest so comfortably now... you can begin to drift even deeper, guided by your imagination...
V.2
I once heard about someone who discovered a very simple way to go deeper into relaxation. On a quiet afternoon, in one of those modern buildings with tall glass windows that let in soft light. They found themselves standing on the third floor, and in the distance they could see an escalator that moved slowly downward.
V.3
When they got closer they could see this was a nice shiny brand new and clean escalator. They stepped onto the gentle escalator and it took them down silently and the speed at which it moved downward, is the speed at which your mind wants to go deeper into relaxation.
V.4
And in your mind's eye when you see that they have reached the next level and they are twice as relaxed as before you can give me a gentle nod.
V.5
And then they noticed another escalator going down to the next level of relaxation, going twice as deep as they were before.
V.6
And as they stepped onto the escalator, it relaxed every muscle in their body as they went down... down... to the next level of relaxation and the speed at which it moved downward, is the speed at which your mind wants to go deeper into relaxation.
V.7
And in your mind's eye, when you see that they have reached the next level and they are twice as relaxed as before, you can give me a gentle nod.
V.8
And finally, they come to another escalator, and this one has a sign that says, 'Deepest Level Below' and this escalator takes them twice as deep and twice as relaxed.
V.9
And then they step onto this escalator and it takes them deeper than they've ever been before into the deepest level of relaxation. and the speed at which it moved downward, is the speed at which your mind wants to go deeper into relaxation.
V.10
And in your mind's eye, when you see that they have reached the last level and they are twice as relaxed as they've ever been before, try to give me a gentle nod if you can.
V.11
And when you notice that they have reached the last level, there is a mirror on the wall as they step down. And in that mirror image, you can see your reflection as you step off of the escalator. And you can notice that all the relaxation that you imagined they experienced was your relaxation.
▶VI. Number Forget Convincer
VI.1
Under hypnosis, you can speak to me if I ask you to.
VI.2
You forgot the number 6. It’s gone from your mind—just disappeared. You know there’s a number between 5 and 7, but it isn’t there now. The harder you try to remember it, the more it fades away.
VI.3
(snap fingers) That number is gone.
VI.4
Now, count out loud from 1 to 10.
VI.5
(pause while they count and skip the missing number)
VI.6
That’s right. That number is completely gone from your mind. Even when you awaken, it stays gone—until I snap my fingers twice.
VI.7
When I snap twice, that number returns instantly, and you count normally again.
VI.8
(snap twice) Good. Everything back to normal now.
VI.9
Count again—1 to 10—perfectly clear.
▶VII. Bridge to Suggestions
VII.1
And now that you’re settled so comfortably… your mind can begin to take in ideas that are helpful and useful for you. You don’t even have to do anything—just notice that the thoughts and words that are good for you can gently settle in, while everything else simply drifts away.
VII.2
In a moment, I’ll begin offering you some helpful suggestions—simple ideas that your mind can absorb easily, in whatever way feels right to you.
VII.3
So as you continue breathing comfortably, these ideas can become a natural part of you…
VII.4
All of these ideas can continue to grow stronger with every breath, every time you practice, every time you think about hypnosis.
