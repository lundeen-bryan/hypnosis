Link: “That comfortable rhythm is the same kind of focus we’ll use now.”
Have you noticed those moments just before sleep when thoughts wander and images appear on their own?
Link: “Hypnosis is like that in‑between state — aware, yet pleasantly absorbed.”
Have you ever been so engaged in a conversation or hobby that time seemed to fly by?
Link: “That natural time-distortion is common in trance as well.”

Tip: One metaphor is usually enough. If it doesn’t land, try a different one — then bridge with and.
