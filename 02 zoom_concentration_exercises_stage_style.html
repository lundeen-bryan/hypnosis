Stage-Style Zoom Concentration Exercises

Direct, performance-style convincers optimized for webcam delivery — light theme • print-friendly

Guidance

Introduce each as a concentration exercise (not a “test”). Use the person’s name, keep tempo brisk, and deliver with confident, rising energy. Demonstrate with your own hands on camera.

Opening line example: “JoeBob, would you like to try a fun concentration exercise?”

  • Camera: Look into the lens during key lines (“Look right at that space…”).
  • Hands visible: Use uncluttered background and good lighting.
  • Voice shape: Count briskly; slow and deepen on the “lock” lines.
  • Reinforce: Celebrate success immediately to build momentum.

1) Magnetic Fingers

Setup

“Alright, JoeBob, let’s try something fun. Clasp your hands tightly together — palms flat, fingers interlaced. Now, extend your two index fingers like this. Good.”

Directions

“In a moment, I’ll count from one to three. On three, separate your index fingers and hold them up like mine — right in front of your eyes. Ready?
One… two… three!”

Suggestion

“Look right at that tiny space between your fingertips. Stare at it.
Imagine magnets pulling those fingers together — pulling, pulling, stronger and stronger now!
The harder you try to keep them apart, the more they pull together. That’s it — closer… closer… and when they touch, that’s right — lock them tight!”

Reinforcement

“Excellent! That shows you’ve got a powerful focus and imagination — exactly what makes hypnosis easy for you. You really have a natural ability for this — you can use your imagination so easily.”

2) Hand Clasp

Setup

“Now, clasp your hands again — fingers locked, palms pressed tight together. Hold them high where I can see them.”

Suggestion

“Squeeze those hands tighter. Tighter still. Feel them pressing together — glue them, weld them, clamp them shut!
The tighter you squeeze, the more they stick. Every second that passes, they lock tighter — tighter — tighter still!”

Locking Phase

“Now, in a moment, I’ll count from one to five.
On five, you’ll find those hands are locked so tight they simply won’t come apart.
One — squeezing tight.
Two — locking like steel.
Three — cemented.
Four — completely stuck.
Five — locked tight! Try to pull them apart — and notice they just hold tighter!”

Release

“Good! Now stop trying, relax… and release!
Hands are free, normal again — and that focus you just used is the same focus that lets you go deep into hypnosis later. You’re a natural at this kind of focus and imagination.”
