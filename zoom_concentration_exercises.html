Zoom Concentration Exercises

Guidance

Introduce each as a concentration exercise (not a “test”). Personalize with the person’s name, and positively reinforce success.

Opening line example: “Lee, would you like to try a fun concentration exercise?”

1) Magnetic Fingers — Concentration Exercise

Setup

  • “Lee, clasp your hands tight together so your palms touch. Now extend your two index fingers like this.” (Demonstrate on camera.)

Directions

  • “In a moment, I’ll count from 1 to 3. On 3, look at your fingers, separate your index fingers, and listen to my voice.”
  • “One… Two… Three.”

Suggestions

  • “Now, Lee, look at your fingers. As you separate them, imagine the space between them getting smaller and smaller, like magnets or rubber bands pulling them together. It may happen slowly, or quickly—either way is fine.”

Reinforce

  • When they touch: “That was great! You’re an amazing hypnotic subject.”

2) Hand Clasp — Concentration Exercise

Setup

  • “Lee, clasp your hands together again, fingers tightly interlocked. Focus on the point where your thumbs cross.”

Locking Suggestions (Count 1–5)

  1. One: “Squeeze your hands tighter and tighter—locking, mashing, melting them together, clamping as if in a vice.”
  2. Two: “As you feel the blood pulsing through your fingers and see the whiteness of your knuckles, they only squeeze and lock tighter and tighter together.”
  3. Three: “Think to yourself: my hands are locked. Let them keep squeezing as if glued, bolted, or magnetized together.”
  4. Four: “Now it becomes reality. Imagine your hands simply cannot come apart. They are locked tightly and firmly together.”
  5. Five: “The harder you try, the tighter they get. Your hands are locked—locked tight together.”

Hold & Release

  • “Now stop trying to take them apart and leave them locked until I release you.”
  • Release line: “...and release! your hands are normal again. Wow—you really are an amazing hypnotic subject!”
