Hypnosis Script with Timer

▶I. Pre-frame

Have you ever noticed how some people close their eyes when they relax?

In a moment, I’ll ask you to open and close your eyes a few times. Each time you close them, it’s simply another chance to relax even more deeply and comfortably.

Is that okay?

▶II. Modified Spiegel Eye Roll Induction w/Fractionation

Some people find it easy to begin hypnosis by simply looking up, just a little higher, as if toward the eyebrows. And as the eyes look upward, there's often a gentle feeling of strain in the eyelids... maybe even behind the eyes.

When you begin to feel that gentle strain on your eyes, that's just a sign for you to naturally allow your eyes to close now. and when you’re ready, to open them again.

Once more, looking upward... feeling that familiar sense returning... letting the eyelids close a little more slowly this time.

...and then, as your eyes open again, this time, let the natural response happen all by itself.

▶III. Eye Catalepsy Convincer

And as those eyelids settle down, you might find that they remain comfortably closed… and every time you repeat that movement, they close even more easily… until they simply prefer to stay closed… allowing you to go deeper and deeper inside.

And in a moment, but not yet, I’m going to ask you to gently test your eyelids to see if they might want to open, but you’re going to find that they juuussst don’t want to open. You can try to test them now. That’s right. Then allow your eyelids to relax. You can stop testing.

▶IV. External Sounds Suggestion

And isn’t it interesting how, the very moment our eyes remain closed, your attention is automatically drawn inward… as though the world outside simply fades, and what matters most is the comfort within.

And any sounds you may hear, or any interruptions or distractions that might occur, can simply be used by your mind as gentle reminders to bring your attention back inward… just as it is now… so that all you really need to notice is the sound of my voice guiding you deeper.

▶V. Spiegel Escalator Deepener

And as those eyelids rest so comfortably now... you can begin to drift even deeper, guided by your imagination...

In a moment, you can begin a gentle descent into a deeper comfort. We'll go down through three levels - A, B, and C.

Each time you descend to the next level, you can drift twice as deep and feel twice as comfortable.

In a moment, but not yet, I'll count from 1 to 3. On 3, you can imagine stepping onto a gentle escalator and begin an easy descent to Level A.

1...2...3... stepping onto the escalator now... letting it move by itself... carrying you down easily and comfortably... and when you arrive at Level A, you can take a deep breath and say the letter “A” out loud.

Good... and in a moment, but not yet... you can move from Level A down to Level B. As you descend, your comfort can easily double, twice as deep, twice as calm as you are right now.

I’ll count from 1 to 3, and on 3, you can drift down to Level B, twice as calm and twice as relaxed as before. 1… 2… 3… drifting down now… deeper and deeper… the escalator carrying you easily and comfortably, all the way to Level B. When you arrive, take a deep breath and say the letter ‘B’ out loud.

Excellent. You're doing very well. There's just one more level left, Level C, which is twice as deep as you are right now. By the time you reach it, you can be more calm and relaxed than you've ever been before. In a moment, I’ll count from 1 to 3. On 3, you can drift down to Level C, even more calm and even more relaxed as before. Don’t try to help it along, just let it happen.

And as you prepare to move from Level B down to Level C, you may find that by the time you reach the last level... your vocal cords will be soooooo relaxed that it may be difficult to say the letter ‘C’
— but try to say it anyway.

1… 2… 3… drifting down now… deeper and deeper… the escalator carrying you easily and comfortably, all the way to Level C.

When you arrive, take a deep breath and try to say the letter ‘C’ out loud, noticing how good it feels to rest there for a moment, completely calm and deeply relaxed.

▶VI. Number Forget Convincer

Under hypnosis, you can speak to me if I ask you to.

You forgot the number 6. It’s gone from your mind—just disappeared. You know there’s a number between 5 and 7, but it isn’t there now. The harder you try to remember it, the more it fades away.

(snap fingers) That number is gone.

Now, count out loud from 1 to 10.

(pause while they count and skip the missing number)

That’s right. That number is completely gone from your mind. Even when you awaken, it stays gone—until I snap my fingers twice.

When I snap twice, that number returns instantly, and you count normally again.

(snap twice) Good. Everything back to normal now.

Count again—1 to 10—perfectly clear.

▶VII. Bridge to Suggestions

And now that you’re settled so comfortably… your mind can begin to take in ideas that are helpful and useful for you. You don’t even have to do anything—just notice that the thoughts and words that are good for you can gently settle in, while everything else simply drifts away.

In a moment, I’ll begin offering you some helpful suggestions—simple ideas that your mind can absorb easily, in whatever way feels right to you.

So as you continue breathing comfortably, these ideas can become a natural part of you…

All of these ideas can continue to grow stronger with every breath, every time you practice, every time you think about hypnosis.

It’s as if the calm and ease you’ve experienced here today stays with you, while you wake up feeling brighter, lighter, and happier than before.
