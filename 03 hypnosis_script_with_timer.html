Modified Spiegel Eye Roll Induction + Escalator Deepener

Pre-frame: In a moment, I’ll ask you to open and close your eyes a few times. Each time you close them, it’s simply another chance to relax even more deeply and comfortably. Until I say the word close, just keep your eyes open and comfortable, noticing what you see and what you feel. When I say close, that’s when you let them close. Is that okay?

Some people find it easy to begin hypnosis by simply looking up, that’s right… just a little higher, as if you’re looking at your eyebrows, so that the eyes begin to notice a gentle strain in the eyelids, and perhaps even behind the eyes.

And as those eyelids feel the strain of looking just a little too high… you might already feel how natural it could be to let them close down now… just for a moment… and then, when you’re ready, to open them again.

(pause while they open their eyes again)

And once more, looking upward again… noticing how familiar that feeling is becoming… and perhaps this time, you could let them drift down even more slowly than before… as if the effort simply melts away with each gentle blink... You can allow your eyelids to close.

(pause, allow them to close slowly, then continue)

Good… and then once again, as your eyes open… drifting upward toward those eyebrows… the very moment you notice that strain… you can allow the natural response to happen all by itself… and this time, it may feel as though the eyes simply want to close… all on their own… as though your unconscious mind already knows how good it feels to just let go.

And as those eyelids settle down, you might find that they remain comfortably closed… and every time you repeat that movement, they close even more easily… until they simply prefer to stay closed… allowing you to go deeper and deeper inside.

And in a moment, but not yet, I’m going to ask you to gently test your eyelids to see if they might want to open but you’re going to find that they juuussst don’t want to open. You can try to test them now. That’s right.Then allow your eyelids to relax.

And isn’t it interesting how, the very moment our eyes remain closed, your attention is automatically drawn inward… as though the world outside simply fades, and what matters most is the comfort within.

And any sounds you may hear, or any interruptions or distractions that might occur, can simply be used by your mind as gentle reminders to bring your attention back inward… just as it is now… so that all you really need to notice is the sound of my voice guiding you deeper.

And as those eyelids rest so comfortably now… you may find yourself ready to go even deeper, with the help of your imagination…

Spiegel Escalator Deepener (Breath Signal at A, B, and C)

When you arrive at Level A, take a big, comfortable breath — just as deeply as feels right for you — and as you let that breath out, notice how that exhale takes you even deeper into comfort and ease.
