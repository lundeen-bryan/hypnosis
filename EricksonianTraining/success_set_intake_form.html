Success Set – Intake Form

1) Identify the Goal

Question: “What is an important goal for you right now?”

Prompt to ask: “On a scale of 1 to 10, how important is achieving this goal for you right now?”

2) Elicit the Motivation

Question: “Why is that important for you?”

3) Presuppose Past Success

Statement: “My intuition says that you’ve experienced this before.”

4) Explore Identity

Question: “When you experienced this, what kind of person were you?”

5) Create a Symbol (Animal Metaphor)

Question: “When you’re like that, which animal comes to mind?”

6) Summarize the State

Prompt: “You want to be … and really feel …”

Example: “You want to be calm, focused, and really feel that steady confidence — like a tiger: relaxed, strong, and ready to act.”
