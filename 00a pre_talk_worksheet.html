"What would you like to get out of today's experience?"

Use either wording:
Direct: “On a scale from 1 to 10, where 10 means it’s severely affecting your life and 1 means it barely matters — how much does this problem impact you right now?”
Rapport‑friendly: “Just so I understand how much this matters to you — if you were to rate how strongly this issue is affecting your life right now, where would it fall on a scale from 1 to 10? Ten being ‘it’s really holding me back’ and one being ‘it’s barely noticeable.’”
