Clean–Ericksonian Hybrid Process

A 7-step guide to discover the client’s metaphor with Clean Language and deliver transformation with Ericksonian storytelling.

1 Prepare the Space (Frame)

Invite comfort, curiosity, and permission. Keep it slow and non-advisory.

As we talk today, I won’t be giving advice — I’ll help you notice what’s already true for you, in your way.
We’ll use your own words as our guide. I’ll ask short questions and give you time to reflect.
2 Invite a Desired Experience

Begin clean. Listen for process words that hint at imagery (stuck, light, open, flowing).

What would you like to have happen?
When that happens, what will you be like?
3 Elicit & Explore the Metaphor (Clean)

Use canonical Clean Language forms; track their exact words.

And what kind of [their word] is that [their word]?
And where is [their word]?
And what happens next?
And is there anything else about [their word]?
4 Develop the Landscape (Still Clean)

Let the metaphor become vivid; watch for spontaneous change (the solution emergence).

And what happens just before [key event]?
And then what happens?
And is there a relationship between [X] and [Y]?

Conditions for Change

Use these to help the client’s unconscious identify what must occur for transformation or movement forward:

What would you like to have happen?
What needs to happen for you to move forward?
Is there anything else that needs to happen for you to get a different result?
5 Transition into Trance (Utilize Their Imagery)

Shift tonality; utilize their words as the induction itself.

And as that [their image] continues, you can notice how a familiar ease begins to spread, the kind you’ve known before, in just the right way for you.
And while your breathing finds its own rhythm, that [their process] can simply keep happening… all by itself.
6 Gift or Amplify the Metaphor (Ericksonian)

If needed, offer a gentle story that harmonizes with their symbols.

Sometimes a traveler who once felt stuck discovers the ground firms with each easy step, as if the earth remembers how to hold them.
And a door that seemed heavy becomes lighter when the hand finds the angle it always knew, and it opens quietly, the way doors do.
7 Integrate & Future-Pace

Invite generalization; keep language permissive and client-led.

And as that image settles in, what lets you know it can stay with you?
And what happens next, as you carry some of this into the places it belongs?
