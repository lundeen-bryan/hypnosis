Modified Spiegel Eye Roll Induction

Dark-mode cue sheet with numbered steps and a continuous script for reading.

Pretalk / Setup

"Hypnosis is simply a way of focusing your attention inward.
All you need to do is follow a few simple steps and notice what happens."

Step 1: Eye Roll

"Without moving your head, look upward — as though you’re trying to see your own eyebrows or the ceiling above you."

Step 2: Noticing Strain

"Notice the gentle strain in your eyelids and forehead…
and the longer you keep them up, the heavier they begin to feel…
almost as if they just want to close all by themselves."

Step 3: Natural Closure

"And sometimes, when we close our eyes,
it feels completely natural to go inside…
to shift your attention inward."

Step 4: Allowing Closure

"So whenever you’re ready, you can just allow your eyelids to close down now…
and as they do, you can follow that natural pull inside,
deeper and more comfortably with each breath."

Step 5: Deepening

"That’s right…
with each easy breath, letting go a little more…
enjoying the comfort that grows as you drift inward."

Step 6: Session Work

[Proceed with suggestions, imagery, or therapeutic process.]

Step 7: Emerging

"In a moment, I’ll count from 1 up to 5.
With each number, you’ll return to full alertness —
1, feeling energy return…
2, becoming more aware of the room…
3, breathing deeply, feeling refreshed…
4, eyes ready to open…
and 5, eyes open now, wide awake, alert, and feeling good."

Continuous Flow Script

"Hypnosis is simply a way of focusing your attention inward. All you need to do is follow a few simple steps and notice what happens. Without moving your head, look upward — as though you’re trying to see your own eyebrows or the ceiling above you. Notice the gentle strain in your eyelids and forehead, and the longer you keep them up, the heavier they begin to feel… almost as if they just want to close all by themselves. And sometimes, when we close our eyes, it feels completely natural to go inside, to shift your attention inward. So whenever you’re ready, you can just allow your eyelids to close down now, and as they do, you can follow that natural pull inside, deeper and more comfortably with each breath. That’s right… with each easy breath, letting go a little more, enjoying the comfort that grows as you drift inward. In a moment, I’ll count from 1 up to 5. With each number, you’ll return to full alertness — 1, feeling energy return… 2, becoming more aware of the room… 3, breathing deeply, feeling refreshed… 4, eyes ready to open… and 5, eyes open now, wide awake, alert, and feeling good."

Tip: Dim your phone’s brightness during sessions. One metaphor in pretalk is usually enough — then bridge with and.
